Pascal Quest was founded by Dr. Pascal Fenkam, a dedicated educator and seasoned entrepreneur with over a decade of experience in AI and computer science. Dr. Fenkam has been leading workshops in schools for more than ten years, organizing and managing computer science clubs, and supervising high school students during their co-op programs. With a strong academic background, he has taught at both the college and university levels, sharing his expertise in software engineering and distributed systems. His passion for education and innovation has also led him to create successful startups, bridging the gap between cutting-edge technology and practical learning.

At Pascal Quest, we specialize in delivering hands-on AI workshops that are highly valued by both educators and students. Our extensive experience includes conducting sessions at esteemed institutions such as Saint Charles Garnier Secondary School, Ronald Marion Secondary School, Monseigneur de Charbonnel Secondary School, École Secondaire Jeune Sans Frontière, and École Norval-Morisseau. We are committed to making AI accessible and easy to understand, helping students simplify their lives through practical AI applications. Whether you are a teacher looking to enhance your curriculum or a student eager to explore the world of AI, Pascal Quest provides the tools and support you need to succeed in today’s digital age.

Our Lab

We provide all the necessary hardware for our workshops, e.g. notebooks, routers, VR and AR, drones, and more. This ensures that both students and educators have access to the tools they need for hands-on learning without any additional setup.

We also include all essential software subscriptions, granting participants access to top-notch technologies. This allows everyone to utilize the latest AI tools seamlessly, enhancing the learning experience without extra costs.

By supplying both hardware and software, we make our workshops highly cost-effective for schools. Our approach eliminates the need for schools to invest in expensive equipment or subscriptions, making high-quality AI education accessible and equitable.

Our Innovations

At Pascal Quest, innovation is at the heart of everything we do. We are dedicated to developing cutting-edge AI applications that enhance learning and enrich lives. One of our flagship projects is Infinicy, an AI biographer designed to collect and curate the cherished memories of loved ones into timeless multimedia stories.

Infinicy transforms personal histories into engaging virtual personalities, audio podcasts, video podcasts, and digital scrapbooks. By preserving these stories, we ensure that every life is celebrated and every narrative is heard. Currently, Infinicy produces high-quality podcasts comparable to NotebookLM, which have garnered significant popularity for their ability to bring memories to life in a meaningful way.

Our commitment to innovation doesn't stop there. We continuously explore new AI-driven solutions to support education and personal storytelling, making advanced technology accessible and impactful for both students and educators. With Pascal Quest’s innovations, the future of AI in education and personal development is bright and full of possibilities.
