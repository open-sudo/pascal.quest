Who Are We

Pascal Quest is led by Dr. Pascal Fenkam, a Solutions Architect at a Fortune 100 company with a PhD and decades of experience in software engineering and distributed systems. As a college professor and innovator, Dr. Fenkam is dedicated to providing practical AI training for students and teachers.

Our workshops are loved by educators and students alike. We have successfully conducted sessions at Saint Charles Garnier Secondary School, Ronald Marion Secondary School, Monsieigneur de Charbonnel Secondary School, and École Secondaire Jeune Sans Frontière. With extensive experience in AI education, Pascal.Quest ensures effective and enjoyable learning experiences.

Our Workshops

1

Introduction to LLM & Generative AI

This session provides teachers and students with a basic understanding of Large Language Models and Generative AI. Learn about different AI applications and master the fundamentals of prompt engineering to effectively use AI in your daily activities and classroom.

2

Exploring Generative AI Tools

Discover and try out popular Generative AI tools like NotebookLM, Gemini Studio, Sora, and Perplexity. This hands-on workshop helps educators and students integrate these tools into their learning processes, enhancing creativity and productivity.

3

Engaging with AI-Powered Physical Devices

Experience AI in action with devices such as Meta Ray Ban, Meta Quest 3, The Frame, OMI.ME, and G1 Even Realities. Learn how these technologies can be used in educational settings to create interactive and engaging learning environments.
